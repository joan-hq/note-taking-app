Features

Everything you need to
retain what you learn

Stop losing valuable insights from your AI conversations. DashNote captures, summarises, and organises it all automatically.

🧩
One-click Chrome Extension
Sync your Gemini conversations directly into DashNote with a single click. No copy-pasting, no friction.
✨
AI Summarisation
Gemini AI distils your chats into structured summaries — key insights, conclusions, and code highlights.
🔍
Full-text Search
Find any note instantly with powerful full-text search and dynamic tag filtering across your knowledge base.
📅
Daily Digest
Get an AI-curated daily summary of what you've learned, delivered to your inbox every morning.
🔐
Google Auth
Sign in securely with your Google account. Your notes are private and always yours.
☁️
Cloud Sync
Deployed on AWS Amplify with PostgreSQL. Access your knowledge base from any device, any time.
How it works

Simple by design

Four steps to never lose a valuable insight again.

01
Install the Extension
Add the DashNote Chrome Extension in seconds. It sits quietly in your browser, ready when you need it.
02
Chat with Gemini
Learn, debug, or explore as usual. When you're done, click the Sync button in your browser.
03
AI Distils Insights
DashNote extracts key knowledge, formats it cleanly, and saves it to your personal knowledge base.
04
Search & Review
Find any insight instantly. Your knowledge compounds and grows more valuable over time.
